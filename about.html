Our Mission

At FurBabies, we're more than just a pet store — we're pet lovers. Our mission is to provide a joyful, affordable, and convenient shopping experience for pet parents. Whether you have dogs, cats, birds, reptiles, or fish, we’ve got the toys, treats, and supplies to keep your companions happy and healthy.

Founded in Lexington, KY, FurBabies has grown from a small local store to a full-service mega shop offering grooming, adoption events, and expert advice — all under one roof.
